"Stone Warrior" earns its ambition: the move from clean arpeggiated guitar into the full-band eruption at the chorus hits with real force, and the lyric turn in the second chorus, "if I crack, even just a bit, I let in the light," reframes the entire setup in a way that lands without feeling cheap. At 123 BPM in F# minor, the track has the momentum of classic arena rock but the emotional specificity of something more personal, closer to early Gaslight Anthem than anything classic-rock radio would have touched. Five minutes is room enough for the idea to breathe, and Lingua Aeternum fills it.
