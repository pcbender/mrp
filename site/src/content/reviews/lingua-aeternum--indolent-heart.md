"Indolent Heart" earns its title honestly: it moves at a deliberate pace that belies its 129 BPM clip, the rhythm engine buried under synth choir and a vocal that stays unhurried right up until the chorus breaks open. That chorus lands well, built on the kind of contrast that makes emotional payoff feel deserved rather than manufactured. The real argument for the track, though, is the breakdown around the 2:17 mark, where the arrangement goes quiet and the lyric "every silence has a spark" sits exposed before the final build comes back in. It's a small structural gamble that pays off.
