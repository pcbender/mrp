In 3/4 time at a waltz-like 117 BPM, 'Altitude' swings wider than you'd expect from a straightforward inspirational rock track, and PCBender earns that ambition. The bridge is where the song clicks hardest: 'Hands off the wheel now, let the horizon turn / Nothing left to prove, nothing left to earn' lands with the kind of plain-spoken conviction you get from John Hiatt at his most direct, and the choir-stacked vocal on the chorus title word turns a simple hook into something that actually fills the room. A highlight.
