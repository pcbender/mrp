"Impossibly" swings for the rafters and mostly clears them: at 123 BPM in B major, the track moves with real urgency, and when that chorus hits, multi-tracked and wide open, the lift is genuine. The lyric earns its ambition too, with a line like "the word becomes the world somehow" landing as hard-won philosophy rather than empty uplift. PCBender's production keeps a dense arrangement from collapsing under its own weight, carving space for the soaring lead guitar against strings and layered vocals in a way that calls to mind the grandest moments of late-period U2 or Springsteen going full stadium. A standout cut.
