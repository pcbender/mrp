The line 'cracked beliefs I cradle like they're clear' is the kind of image that earns its keep, and PCBender builds the whole song around that tension between resignation and stubborn hope, letting the verses sit quietly in F# major before the chorus breaks open into something genuinely anthemic. At 112 BPM the pacing feels deliberate, almost patient, and the production rewards that restraint: the moment the wall of sound arrives, it lands with real weight. The two-word payoff at the end, 'Because YOU do,' is a gutsy simplification after all that lyrical density, and it works.
