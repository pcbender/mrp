Running at a breathless 172 BPM in E minor, 'Tits Up' is the kind of breakup song that doesn't ask for sympathy, it demands the exit. The distorted guitar wall and punchy rhythm section hit immediately, but it's the chorus that earns its keep: 'Took my heart and fucked it up' lands with the blunt economy of early Alanis Morissette, and the final twist, 'imagine me tits down on your best friend,' is the line that'll stick with you long after the 3:02 runtime. PCBender keeps the production loud and unified without burying the vocal, which cuts through with real attitude. A clean, catchy gut-punch of a track.
