Written in 3/4 and built around a lyric that earns its weight, 'You Become the Night' opens with clean, delayed guitar and a quiet sense of reckoning before the chorus hits like a door blown open. The line 'the fears overtake but the darkness provides' is the kind of paradox that lands differently depending on where you are in life, and PCBender lets it breathe. That dynamic shift from introspection into the full-band surge is the track's best move, and he doesn't waste it.
