Rose builds this one like a preacher who also plays arenas: the opening comes in clean and spacious, then the full arrangement drops at the chorus around the one-minute mark and the room gets noticeably bigger. F# major at a clip just over 160 BPM gives the whole thing a restless, forward-leaning energy that suits the lyric perfectly, 'crossing with a certain sense of uncertainty' being the kind of line that sounds obvious until you realize it's the truest thing on the track. The guitar work in the back half earns its space, and the chorus hook lands with the same chest-forward confidence you get from peak-era Tom Petty. A highlight.
