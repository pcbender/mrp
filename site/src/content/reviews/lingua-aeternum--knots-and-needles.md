At 117.5 BPM in D major, 'Knots and Needles' moves with the kind of locked-in, forward-leaning drive that recalls early Silversun Pickups before they got too polished, and the lyric 'the knot does not the needle drop' lands as the sort of knotty, plain-spoken refrain that earns its repetition. The production is genuinely spacious, letting the guitars push without crowding the vocal, and the final minute delivers on everything the song sets up, that repeated 'the needle needs the knot to thread the needle' building into a full-room swell that closes the loop. A highlight.
