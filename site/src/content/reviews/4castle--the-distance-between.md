The central lyric here does real work: 'You mean what I say, I say what you mean / each preaching insight but not what's been' captures the specific exhaustion of two people talking past each other while convinced they're connecting, and 4Castle builds a track around it that earns the sentiment. At a steady 107 BPM in G major, the song settles into a groove that feels like a conversation finding its rhythm, and the guitar solo that crests around the two-minute mark is the kind of melodic, purposeful climb that reminds you why the instrument matters in rock. 'The Distance Between' lands cleanly.
