The remix of 'Tits Up' arrives lean and loud at just under three minutes, and it earns every second of that runtime. The chorus at 0:42 is the centre of gravity: gang vocals pile onto the hook 'took my heart and fucked it up' with enough weight to suggest early Paramore crashing a Bangles reunion, and the distorted guitars sit wide enough in the mix that the whole thing feels physically bigger than its parts. At 89 BPM in E minor, the tempo keeps the indignation believable rather than hysterical, and the closing lyric, 'imagine me tits down on your best friend,' lands as the best kind of mic-drop, a punchline that earns its place. A highlight.
