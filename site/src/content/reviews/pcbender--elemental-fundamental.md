PCBender plants 'Elemental, Fundamental' in F# major and lets it breathe, opening with just piano and a close, warm vocal before the arrangement earns its grandeur section by section. By the time the chorus lands, 'we were born in breath and rhythm / and the song has made us whole,' the swelling harmonies feel less like a production choice and more like a logical conclusion, the way a Springsteen anthem makes the build feel inevitable. At 143 BPM the track still never rushes; it carries the weight of something meant to last. A highlight.
