"Bobo's Lair" earns its title as an anthem by actually building like one: the verses sit in a slow, measured D# minor groove at 83 BPM, clean guitars ringing with space, before the chorus hits like a door blowing open and 'Feel the thunder, hear it calling, deep inside my soul ignites' lands with genuine weight. That dynamic lift around the one-minute mark is the kind of moment 4Castle does better than almost anyone in their lane, closer in spirit to early Heart or prime-era Whitesnake than anything on indie radio right now. The lyric occasionally leans on its archetypes a little hard, but the conviction in the performance makes the familiar imagery feel earned rather than borrowed. A highlight.
