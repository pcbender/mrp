PCBender has always had a feel for the big emotional swing, and 'Memory Assembly' is him swinging for the seats. The lyric 'Will we remember this? / We already do' lands with the kind of quiet certainty that good songwriting earns rather than announces, and the chorus delivery earns it: a clean, arpeggiated intro that opens into a wide, layered swell where the vocals and guitars stack into something genuinely anthemic. At a brisk 143 BPM in E minor, it never feels hurried, just purposeful, the way a Springsteen mid-tempo builds before it crests. A highlight, and one of his most accessible entries.
