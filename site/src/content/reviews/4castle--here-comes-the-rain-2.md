At 89 BPM in C# minor, 'Here Comes the Rain 2' builds its case slowly and then all at once: the verses stay low and grounded, guitars locked tight with the rhythm section, before the chorus opens up into something genuinely cathartic, the kind of wide-sky release that early Foo Fighters used to nail without making it feel calculated. The production is clean enough to let every layer breathe while still hitting hard, and that dynamic contrast is the track's real argument for itself. A confident instrumental statement from a band that knows how to make a room feel something.
