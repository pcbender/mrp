The hook lands hard: 'I am darkness with no place to hide / From the piercing dread of light' is the kind of chorus that earns its capital letters, and in G# minor at a steady 112 BPM the song has the muscular forward pull of classic arena rock without the self-congratulation. What sells it is the twist at the bridge, where the narrator stops claiming power and admits 'I am not a force / I'm the failure of one' -- that deflation is the emotional payoff the whole track is building toward, and the layered backing vocals that lift the final chorus feel genuinely earned rather than decorative. Lingua Aeternum is playing a bigger room here than usual, and they fill it.
