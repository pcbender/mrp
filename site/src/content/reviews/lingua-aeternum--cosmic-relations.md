At 161 BPM in A# minor, 'Cosmic Relations' moves fast and hits hard, with a chorus that opens up like a pressure valve releasing: 'The Gods align, they trace the vibrations' lands with the kind of lift you get from early U2 at their most unambiguous. The verses do real work too, stacking elemental imagery ('Steel on bone, heartbeat loud / Victory dances beneath red cloud') before each explosion into that anthemic refrain. The production earns its density, letting the vocal cut through the full wall of guitars and drums rather than getting swallowed by it. A highlight in their catalog, and a strong entry point for anyone new to the circle.
