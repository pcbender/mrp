Four tracks, fourteen minutes, and a BPM arc that deliberately winds down from the opener's mid-tempo churn to the closer's slow exhale: this EP knows exactly what it is doing with its brief runtime. 'Invocate' is the hinge the whole thing turns on, its locked-in forward drive giving the collection its pulse before 'The Memory I'll Borrow' eases back into something looser and more reflective. The thematic thread, words like 'echo,' 'time,' and 'bends' surfacing across all four tracks, keeps the set feeling less like a playlist and more like a single sustained thought. 'Thread by Thread' lands the EP quietly rather than conclusively, that chorus hook about what's broken speaking loudest doing just enough to make the fadeout feel earned rather than unfinished.
