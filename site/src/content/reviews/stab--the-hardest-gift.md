At 143 BPM in E minor, 'The Hardest Gift' doesn't waste a second getting to the point: STAB opens on a thick wall of guitars and a rhythm section that locks in and doesn't let go. The chorus lands the way a good breakup-as-victory song should, with the central line 'losing you was the hardest gift' doing real emotional work without overselling it. It's the kind of hook that earns its stadium-ready production, recalling the cleaner end of early Paramore or a punchier Letters to Cleo. At three minutes and thirteen seconds it's tight, purposeful, and a highlight.
