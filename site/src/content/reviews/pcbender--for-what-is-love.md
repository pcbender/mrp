PCBender builds this one from almost nothing, a fingerpicked acoustic and a close-miked vocal that feels genuinely private, then earns every instrument it adds until the full arrangement arrives like a door swinging open. The lyric structures love as a single life in four movements, morning through night through end, and the payoff line 'It's love we shared that matters most' lands because the song has done the work to get there. At 117 BPM in C major, the tempo sits right at the edge of hymn and rock anthem, which is exactly where this belongs.
