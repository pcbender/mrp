At 143 BPM in a bright E major, 'Ramps and Lanes' keeps moving the way its lyrics insist you should: forward, by choice, not by force. The central hook, 'Not the path but the choosing,' lands with the kind of clean conviction that early Counting Crows used to manage before everything got complicated, and the arpeggiated guitar work underneath it earns every bit of the lift the chorus promises. It does its job and does it well.
