PCBender builds 'How Many Gone?' the old-fashioned way: verses that breathe, a chorus that earns its weight, and a lyric that lands without straining for poetry. 'The mirror hums a quiet tune / Of faces blurred by what they've known' is the kind of line that stops you mid-listen, and the song's E minor pulse at a steady near-100 BPM gives it the feel of a long road actually being walked. The dynamic shift after the reflective bridge, full band crashing back in with those soaring guitars, hits the way a Springsteen album cut does when the studio finally opens up. A highlight.
