PCBender keeps the premise simple and lets the hook do the heavy lifting: a guy who has the girl but not really, waiting by the phone in A major at a clip just north of 143 BPM that feels more urgent than the tempo suggests. The verses do their job setting up the emotional math, but the chorus is where the track earns its keep, that tripled 'have a heart' refrain hitting with the kind of cathartic release you get from early Cheap Trick or late-period Fountains of Wayne. The production packs the guitars dense and wide without burying the vocal, and the lift into the chorus lands every time.
