A waltz-time rock build in F# minor at a crisp 123 BPM, 'Crimson Decision 2' earns its title: that piano arpeggiation in the opening fifteen seconds feels genuinely tense, and when the full band drops at the 0:15 mark, the payoff is immediate and clean.
