In 3/4 time at a steady 117 BPM, 'Aim for True' turns the act of creative struggle into something you can actually feel in your chest. The lyric 'the hand still shakes, the work's not done / you let it go and start again' lands with the plainspoken weight of a John Hiatt confession, and the chorus hook earns every repeat through sheer economy: two words, no argument. The production balances drive and restraint well, keeping the mix clear even as the arrangement fills out, and the waltz meter gives the whole thing an inevitability that suits the theme. A highlight, and one of their more immediate offerings.
