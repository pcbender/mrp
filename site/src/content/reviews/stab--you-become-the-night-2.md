At 112 BPM in 3/4, 'You Become the Night' moves with the unhurried sway of a slow waltz that knows exactly when to detonate. The structure earns its nearly five minutes: sparse, reverbed guitar opens things quietly in B major before the full band crashes in with a wall of distortion that lands like early Heart at full tilt. That dynamic contrast, whisper to roar and back again across twelve distinct sections, is the whole argument of the track, and STAB makes it convincingly. A highlight.
