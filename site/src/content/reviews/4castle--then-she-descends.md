A waltz in G# major at a measured 112 BPM, 'Then She Descends' earns its title by actually delivering the drop it promises: the verses hold so much back, a spare clean guitar and a voice carrying lines like 'every hour stretches longer / until she comes at last,' that when the chorus opens up with swelling synths and the full kit coming in, it genuinely feels like the room expanding. The lyric 'a broken man who dares to stand' lands because the song has done the emotional work to get you there. This is 4Castle playing the long game, and the patience pays off.
