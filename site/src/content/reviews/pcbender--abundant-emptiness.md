At 95 BPM in B minor, 'Abundant Emptiness' settles into a groove that feels like a long exhale, clean bluesy guitar lines carrying the verse with room to breathe before the chorus opens up into something genuinely anthemic. The lyric 'a moment, not a place' earns its weight, and PCBender delivers it with the kind of quiet conviction that makes the payoff land. Production is spacious without being sparse, and the dynamic lift into the title phrase gives the track its spine. A highlight in the catalog.
