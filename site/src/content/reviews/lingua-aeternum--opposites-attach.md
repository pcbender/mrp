Running in 3/4 at a brisk 117 BPM, 'Opposites Attract' finds Lingua Aeternum in full anthem mode: arpeggiated clean guitars build the verse with patience, then the chorus detonates into wide, distorted power chords that genuinely earn the release. The lyric pairs are almost liturgical in their sweep, 'breath and silence, living and dead,' and the waltz feel keeps that catalog of dualities from feeling like a list, giving each line a small, rolling momentum. The production is smart enough to keep the vocal reverb airy even when the guitars are stacked thick, so the climax breathes where a lesser mix would just compress. A highlight.
