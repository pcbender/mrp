At 129 BPM in A major, this one moves with a restrained urgency that pays off the moment the chorus opens up and PCBender lets the full band breathe. The lyric 'I am the archer and the string, my arrow's missing fletch' is the kind of line that earns a second listen on its own, and the production gives it room, clean and spacious without feeling sterile. The dynamic arc from those measured, searching verses into the anthemic release of 'Become the lie you haven't met' is the track's real argument, and it wins the case. A highlight.
