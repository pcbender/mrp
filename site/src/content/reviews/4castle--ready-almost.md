At 92 BPM in C major, 'Ready Almost' moves with the patient confidence of a band that knows the payoff is coming, and it is: the chorus at 1:23 hits like a stadium opening up, layered vocals and crashing cymbals landing with the kind of cathartic weight that puts you in mind of early Heart at their most anthemic. The verses hold back well, the baritone lead sitting in controlled tension over a crunchy rhythm bed before everything opens wide. Twelve sections in under five minutes means the architecture is doing real work here, and 4Castle earns every bit of it.
