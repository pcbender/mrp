At 129 BPM in B minor, 'Measure's Measure' builds like a classic arena rock track that knows exactly when to open up: the shift from reflective verse into full-band chorus delivers the kind of emotional release that early U2 chased on their bigger records. The lyric 'we count, but never capture the song that will not fade' is the kind of line that earns its moment, and at six-plus minutes PCBender gives it room to breathe rather than overstaying its welcome. The shimmering arpeggios against the distorted rhythm guitars is a production choice that pays off every time the chorus kicks in. A genuine highlight.
