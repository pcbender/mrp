At 143 BPM in a bright E major, this one moves faster than it feels, the piano and delayed guitars holding things open and airy while the rhythm section quietly builds underneath. The lyric does real work: 'It isn't science / It isn't retreat / It's love / Where sky and morning meet' earns its plainness because the song has set up the image patiently, letting sunrise stand in for something harder to say. The swell that arrives around the 1:17 mark lands the way a good Tom Petty chorus lands, not flashy, just inevitable. A highlight.
