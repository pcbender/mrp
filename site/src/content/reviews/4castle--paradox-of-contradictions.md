Following the full-force momentum of 'Haymakers and Body Blows,' 'Paradox of Contradictions' does something the album quietly needs: it eases the throttle without killing the energy. At a relaxed mid-tempo in a bright major key, the central lyric, 'a puzzle without the pieces, the lady for me,' is the kind of line that earns its repetition, landing differently each time the chorus swings back around. The production gives every voice room to breathe, and when the harmonies stack up for the final chorus push, the band earns that arena-sized space they're reaching for. Taken alone it reads as a grower that rewards the full listen; in sequence it's the hinge the album's second half pivots on, absorbing the weight of what came before and leaving the record open enough for the stylistic shift that follows.
