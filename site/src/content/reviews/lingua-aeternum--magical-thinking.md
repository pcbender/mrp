At 123 BPM in F minor, 'Magical Thinking' builds its argument the same way its lyrics do: brick by brick, with conviction. The interlocking guitar and synth arpeggios that open the track give way to a punchy rhythm section that makes the chorus land like a genuine statement, and the line 'I don't obey, I architect' is the kind of moment that earns the whole setup. The dynamic contrast between the almost clockwork precision of the instrumental motifs and the impassioned vocal delivery keeps it from feeling too tidy, and the climactic final chorus pays off everything the verses promised.
