A wordless anthem built around a single repeated vocal hook, 'In and out and over through,' this track earns its runtime by treating that phrase as a structural principle rather than a shortcut. PCBender lets the opening piano breathe for a full minute before the electronic beat and swelling synths arrive, and when they do, the payoff is real, the kind of controlled release that Elbow used to nail on their best mid-2000s records. At 99 BPM in C major, the pace is deliberate without dragging, and the mix stays clean and wide throughout all eight sections without ever feeling overcrowded. A confident instrumental that holds the room.
