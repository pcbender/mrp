At 89 BPM in C# minor, 'You Still Have My Heart' is built around the push and pull between a tight, chugging verse and a chorus that opens up like a door blown off its hinges, the line 'like a northern star, you guide me where you are' landing exactly where it needs to. STAB's vocal sits high in the mix with real authority, cutting through the wide, distorted guitars without losing the ache underneath. It's the kind of power-ballad architecture that Fleetwood Mac perfected and Heart weaponized, and this sits comfortably in that lineage.
