"Attunement" is a quieter corner of 4Castle's catalog, built on a sustained drone and breathy lead vocal that lets the lyric breathe at its own pace, and the writing earns that patience: "A note unsung hums within me / A longing. A drawn bow. A vow" is the kind of line that lands harder on the third listen than the first. The harmonic swell around the one-minute mark, where the voices thicken briefly before pulling back, is the emotional center of a track that otherwise trusts silence to do the work. At just under two and a half minutes in B major, it reads more like a devotional sketch than a full statement, which may leave some listeners wanting more architecture, but for the faithful it is a grower worth sitting with.
