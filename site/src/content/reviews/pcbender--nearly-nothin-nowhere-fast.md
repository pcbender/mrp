PCBender has a gift for turning existential drift into something you can two-step to, and 'Nearly Nothin', Nowhere Fast' is that gift in full swing. The hook earns its keep: 'a jukebox heart stuck on the spins' is the kind of line that lands on first listen and sticks around, and the E minor groove at 117 BPM keeps the whole thing moving with exactly the restless urgency the lyric calls for. The slide guitar solo that arrives midway through is the real standout, a fiery, melodic centerpiece that does the emotional work the verses set up, somewhere between early Allman Brothers and a sunburned John Hiatt. A highlight.
