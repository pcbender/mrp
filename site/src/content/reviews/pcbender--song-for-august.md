The trick PCBender pulls off here is structural: the chorus doesn't just repeat, it evolves, so by the time the final version lands with 'I seem to find the tempo' flipping the lyric on its axis, the resolution feels earned rather than announced. At 89 BPM in A minor, the track moves with a measured urgency, and that dynamic break around the 53-second mark, where the arrangement opens wide and the rhythm section locks in hard, is the moment the song stops being good and starts being convincing. 'The desert hums forgiveness / for what it understands' is the kind of line that justifies the whole four minutes. A highlight.
