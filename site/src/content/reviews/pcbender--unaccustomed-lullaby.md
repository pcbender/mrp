The lyric does something quietly brave: it gives a voice to the lullaby itself, a melody that exists only in the margins, "unheard" and "ridiculed," finding its purpose anyway. That conceit could tip into sentimentality, but the clean fingerpicked guitar and a vocal that stays small and close to the ear keep it honest. The chorus hook lands on the right word every time, and "a thread in the darkness, a soft silver rune" is the kind of line that earns its place. A highlight, and a patient one.
