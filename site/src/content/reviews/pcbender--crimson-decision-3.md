Thirteen sections across four and a half minutes sounds like a lot to manage, and PCBender earns every one of them: 'Crimson Decision 3' builds from a pensive, open-voiced G-major guitar figure at a measured 107 BPM into something that genuinely pushes against its own restraint before the full arrangement lands. The layered electric and acoustic guitars stay wide without going muddy, and the moment the rhythm section commits is the track's best argument for itself. For an instrumental, it holds attention the way a good film cue does, propelling forward rather than decorating. A highlight.
