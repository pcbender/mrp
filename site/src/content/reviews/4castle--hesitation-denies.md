At 143 BPM in A minor, 'Hesitation Denies' earns its title by refusing to rush: the verses sit spare and close, just a vocal and a breath of guitar, before the chorus opens up into something genuinely cathartic, with all five voices locking into that line 'the spirit dares to fly' like they've been waiting three minutes to say it. The payoff lands because 4Castle trusts the contrast, keeping the build honest rather than telegraphing it. A highlight.
