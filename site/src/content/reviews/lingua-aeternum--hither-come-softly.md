"The soul's first scar is the soul's first kiss" lands the way the best chorus lines do: you hear it once and feel like you already knew it. In C# minor and moving at a crisp 123 BPM, the track earns its anthemic lift the honest way, building from spare, clean verses into a wide, punchy chorus that feels like relief rather than bombast. It's the kind of song that reminds you of early Coldplay at their most direct, before the production got too tidy. A highlight.
