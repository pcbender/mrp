A waltz-time anthem in F major that earns its optimism the old-fashioned way: a verse that actually sets something up before the chorus pays it off. The full-band kick at 0:48 lands with the kind of lift you get from early Tom Petty, and lines like 'the seeds you plant will one day grow' stay on the honest side of inspirational without tipping into greeting card. At 2:39 it knows exactly when to leave.
