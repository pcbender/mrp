At 89 BPM in E major, 'Yesterday's Delayed' builds its case slowly and then detonates right around the 53-second mark, where the guitars widen out and the vocal lifts into the hook with a conviction that earns the release. The lyric 'the truth is wrapped in riddles, a lock without a key' does exactly what a chorus setup should: it makes the resigned refrain of 'never finding, yesterday delayed' land as something felt rather than stated. PCBender has always known how to let a groove breathe before he turns it loose, and this track is a clean example of that instinct paying off. Fans of early Tom Petty's more searching mid-tempo work will find familiar footing here.
