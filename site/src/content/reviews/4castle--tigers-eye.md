'Tiger's Eye' arrives at the midpoint of the album's first half, and it does exactly what a fifth track should: it anchors. At a deliberate pace, the build from close-mic'd intimacy to full-band release is handled with real craft, and when the hook lands on 'steady flame when I get lost inside,' the vocal stack blooms into something that recalls Heart at their most unguarded. The lyric earns its directness too, with lines like 'you don't tell me how to be / but somehow, you still set me free' threading vulnerability without tipping into sentiment. Coming after the airy restraint of 'By Starlight Drifting West,' this one plants its feet, and the emotional steadiness it projects sets up the more combative energy that follows.
